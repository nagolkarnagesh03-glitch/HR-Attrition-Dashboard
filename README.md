# 👩‍💼 HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition and workforce trends using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into key factors influencing employee turnover and helps organizations improve retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition patterns
* Identify key factors affecting employee turnover
* Evaluate impact of job satisfaction, work-life balance, and salary
* Provide data-driven insights for HR decision-making

---

## 📂 Dataset Details

The dataset includes employee-level information such as:

* Employee ID, Age, Gender
* Job Role, Job Level, Education Level
* Monthly Income, Years at Company
* Work-Life Balance, Job Satisfaction, Performance Rating
* Remote Work, Leadership Opportunities, Innovation Opportunities
* Company Reputation, Employee Recognition
* Attrition (0 = Stayed, 1 = Left)

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers for interactivity
* KPI Metrics (Attrition Rate, Total Employees, Avg Income, Avg Tenure)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Conditional Formatting

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 👥 Total Employees & Attrition Count
* 📉 Attrition Rate (%)
* 👨‍💼 Attrition by Job Role
* 👩‍💻 Attrition by Gender & Age Group
* ⚖️ Work-Life Balance Impact on Attrition
* 😊 Job Satisfaction Analysis
* 🏠 Remote Work vs Attrition
* 🎛 Interactive Filters (Job Role, Education, Marital Status, Gender)

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Higher attrition observed among younger employees
* Employees with poor work-life balance tend to leave more
* Job satisfaction has a strong impact on employee retention
* Certain job roles show significantly higher attrition rates
* Remote work shows variation in attrition behavior

---



## 💡 Conclusion

This dashboard enables HR teams to identify attrition trends and take proactive steps to improve employee satisfaction and retention.
